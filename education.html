Education

Education is a process of imparting knowledge from teachers to the students. This process may contain a different variety of steps depending on the stage and quality of education. It is a key process which plays a significant role in maintaining the culture and ethics of the society.

According to Census 2011, the total population of the village was 17088 and 2947 houses, whereas the literacy rate was about 68.20% which is slightly lower than India's literacy rate of 74%.But today when you visit this village, you will see that the schools and coaching institutes have grown significantly. Government Schools have renovated. Parents are becoming aware of education. As a result, many students of the village are getting jobs.
